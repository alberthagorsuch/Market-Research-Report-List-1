<p><p>The PCR reagent market comprises various essential components that facilitate the polymerase chain reaction process. Primers are short DNA sequences that initiate amplification. Enzymes, specifically DNA polymerases, synthesize new DNA strands. DNTPs (deoxynucleotide triphosphates) provide the building blocks for DNA synthesis. The template is the DNA sample to be amplified, while buffer solutions maintain optimal conditions for the reaction. Each component plays a critical role in ensuring accurate and efficient PCR, driving demand in research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
