<p><p>The semiconductor chip handler market comprises several types, including pick-and-place handlers, turret handlers, gravity handlers, and others. Pick-and-place handlers automate the positioning of chips for testing and packaging. Turret handlers utilize rotating platforms to optimize throughput and efficiency. Gravity handlers rely on gravitational force to align chips for testing, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Other specialized handlers cater to unique requirements, such as specific chip sizes or complex handling processes, enhancing overall production capabilities in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
