<p><p>The Augmented Reality (AR) market can be categorized into three main types: Head-Mounted, Head-Up, and Handheld. Head-Mounted displays, like AR glasses, immerse users in a digital experience while keeping their hands free. Head-Up displays project information onto surfaces, often enhancing real-world tasks without obstructing the view. Handheld augmented reality utilizes smartphones or tablets to overlay digital content on the real world, making it widely accessible and popular for applications like gaming and navigation. Each type offers unique user interactions and experiences.</p></p>
