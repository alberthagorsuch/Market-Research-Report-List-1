<p><p>Horizontal acid pumps are designed for transferring corrosive fluids, with two main types: multi-stage and single-stage pumps. Multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers, allowing for higher pressure and flow rates, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, single-stage pumps have a single impeller, providing simpler designs for lower pressure tasks. Both types are crucial in industries like chemical processing, where efficient and safe transport of acids and aggressive fluids is essential for operations.</p></p>
