<p><p>Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation (HSCT) involves infusing healthy stem cells to restore blood and immune function in patients with blood disorders. The market is primarily divided into two types: autologous transplant, where patients receive their own stem cells after treatment, and allogenic transplant, involving stem cells from a compatible donor. Autologous transplants generally have fewer complications but may not be suitable for all conditions, while allogenic transplants provide a broader potential for cure but come with greater risks.</p></p>
