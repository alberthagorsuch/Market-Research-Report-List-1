<p><p>Vein stripping guidewires are categorized into two primary market types: disposable and reusable. Disposable guidewires are designed for single-use, ensuring sterility and eliminating the risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for outpatient procedures. Reusable guidewires, on the other hand, are crafted for multiple uses, often subjected to rigorous sterilization between uses, which can be more cost-effective in high-volume settings. Both types play a crucial role in vascular procedures, providing necessary support and access during vein stripping surgeries.</p></p>
