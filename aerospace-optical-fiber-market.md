<p><strong>The Aerospace Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Muti Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace optical fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: single mode and multimode fibers. Single mode fibers feature a small core diameter, allowing for precise light transmission over long distances with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for high-performance applications in aerospace. In contrast, multimode fibers have a larger core, enabling multiple light paths, which is beneficial for shorter distance communications. Both types are crucial for enhancing data transfer capabilities, reliability, and efficiency in aerospace systems.</p></p>
