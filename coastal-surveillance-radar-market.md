<p><p>Coastal surveillance radar is categorized into several types, primarily X-Band and S-Band radars. X-Band radars operate at higher frequencies, providing high-resolution imagery ideal for detecting small vessels and facilitating precise tracking. In contrast, S-Band radars function at lower frequencies, offering longer-range capabilities and better performance in adverse weather conditions, making them suitable for broader area coverage. Other types may include multifrequency or innovative integrated systems that combine various technologies to enhance coastal monitoring and security.</p></p>
