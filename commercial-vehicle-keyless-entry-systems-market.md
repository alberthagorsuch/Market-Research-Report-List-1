<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Keyless Entry Systems market can be divided into two primary types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES enables vehicles to unlock automatically as the authorized driver approaches, providing convenience and enhancing security. In contrast, RKES requires users to press a button on a remote fob to unlock or start the vehicle. Both systems enhance user experience while ensuring vehicle security, catering to the evolving needs of the commercial transportation sector.</p></p>
