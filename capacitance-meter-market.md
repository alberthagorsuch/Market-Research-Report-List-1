<p><p>Capacitance meters are essential tools for measuring capacitance in electronic components. They are categorized into two main types: non-bridge meters and bridge meters. Non-bridge meters offer a straightforward design, providing direct capacitance readings through simple digital displays, making them user-friendly for quick measurements. In contrast, bridge meters utilize a more complex setup, allowing for accurate comparison of unknown capacitance against a known reference, thereby enhancing precision in high-performance applications. Both types cater to different user needs and measurement complexities.</p></p>
