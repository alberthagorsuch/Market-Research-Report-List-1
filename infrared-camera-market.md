<p><ul><li>Cooled infrared detectors</li><li>Uncooled infrared detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared camera market features two primary types of detectors: cooled and uncooled infrared detectors. Cooled infrared detectors use cryogenic cooling systems to enhance sensitivity, making them suitable for high-performance applications like military and scientific research due to their ability to detect faint thermal signals. Conversely, uncooled infrared detectors operate at ambient temperatures, offering faster response times and lower costs, making them ideal for commercial applications such as surveillance, automotive night vision, and building inspections.</p></p>
