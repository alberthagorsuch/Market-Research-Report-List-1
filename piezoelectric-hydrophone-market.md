<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Hydrophone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scalar Hydrophone</li><li>Vector Hydrophone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric hydrophone market includes scalar and vector hydrophones, each serving distinct applications in underwater acoustics. Scalar hydrophones measure pressure changes in the water, providing overall sound intensity and amplitude information. In contrast, vector hydrophones capture both pressure and particle motion, offering detailed directional data and insights into underwater sound fields. These technologies are crucial in marine research, underwater communication, and military applications, enabling precise monitoring and analysis of acoustic environments.</p></p>
