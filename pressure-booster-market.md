<p><p>The pressure booster market can be categorized into three main types. Volumetric pressure boosters utilize positive displacement to increase fluid pressure, making them suitable for specific applications that require consistent pressure. Centrifugal pressure boosters rely on rotating impellers to generate pressure, offering efficiency for larger flow rates in various industrial processes. Other types encompass specialized boosters designed for niche applications, including hybrid systems and those tailored for particular fluids or operational conditions, expanding the versatility of pressure boosting solutions.</p></p>
