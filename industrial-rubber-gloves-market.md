<p><p>The industrial rubber gloves market comprises several key types, each serving specific applications. Natural Rubber (Latex) gloves offer excellent elasticity and comfort but may cause allergies in some users. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) gloves are valued for their cost-effectiveness and chemical resistance but lack the same durability. Nitrile gloves provide a robust alternative with great puncture resistance and low allergenic potential. Neoprene gloves combine flexibility with resistance to a variety of chemicals and temperatures. Others may include specialty materials designed for niche applications.</p></p>
