<p><ul><li>Contact Card</li><li>Contactless Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart card technologies encompass two primary market types: contact and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical insertion into a reader, establishing a direct connection for data exchange, often used in banking and identification systems. Conversely, contactless cards utilize RFID or NFC technology, allowing for quick, tap-and-go transactions without physical contact. This makes them ideal for applications like public transportation and retail payments, offering convenience and speed. Both types enhance security and user experience across various sectors.</p></p>
