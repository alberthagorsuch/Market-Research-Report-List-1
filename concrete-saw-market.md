<p><p>The concrete saw market is primarily divided into two main types: handheld cut-off saws and walk-behind push saws. Handheld cut-off saws are portable tools designed for making precise cuts in concrete, often used for smaller projects or repairs. In contrast, walk-behind push saws are larger, more powerful machines suitable for heavy-duty applications, enabling operators to make deeper and wider cuts efficiently. Both types cater to different needs in construction, renovation, and maintenance tasks, enhancing versatility in concrete work.</p></p>
