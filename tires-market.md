<p><p>The tire market primarily features radial and bias tires. Radial tires have layers of fabric cords running perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, durability, and grip, making them popular for modern vehicles. Bias tires, with crisscrossed cord layers, are stiffer and more robust, often used for heavy-duty or off-road applications. While radial tires dominate the market due to their performance advantages, bias tires remain relevant in specific industries requiring ruggedness and load-bearing capacity.</p></p>
