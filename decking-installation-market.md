<p><p>The decking installation market is primarily divided into two key types: composite and PVC. Composite decking, made from a blend of wood fibers and plastic, offers a natural wood appearance with enhanced durability and low maintenance. It is often preferred for its eco-friendly attributes. PVC decking, on the other hand, is entirely plastic, providing superior resistance to moisture, stains, and fading, making it ideal for high-humidity environments. Both types are popular for residential and commercial applications due to their longevity and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
