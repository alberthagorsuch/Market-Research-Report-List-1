<p><p>Ingestible medical devices encompass various types, each serving distinct diagnostic purposes. Temperature devices monitor internal body heat for fever or infection detection. Pressure devices assess gastrointestinal pressure, aiding in disorders like gastroparesis. pH devices measure acidity levels within the gastrointestinal tract, crucial for diagnosing conditions like acid reflux. Image devices capture visual data, allowing for real-time imaging of the digestive system. Together, these technologies enhance non-invasive diagnostics, improving patient outcomes and broadening clinical insights.</p></p>
