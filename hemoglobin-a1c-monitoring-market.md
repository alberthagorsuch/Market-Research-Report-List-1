<p><strong>The Hemoglobin A1c Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion-exchange</li><li>High-performance liquid chromatography (HPLC)</li><li>Immunoassay</li><li>Boronate affinity chromatography</li><li>Direct enzymatic assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hemoglobin A1c monitoring market comprises various diagnostic methods to measure blood glucose levels over time. Ion-exchange chromatography separates different hemoglobin variants based on charge. High-performance liquid chromatography (HPLC) offers precise quantification by separating components at high speed. Immunoassays utilize antibodies for specific binding to A1c, enabling accurate measurement. Boronate affinity chromatography selectively binds with glycosylated hemoglobin, while direct enzymatic assays employ enzymes to directly measure glucose-bound hemoglobin. Each method offers unique advantages for effective diabetes management.</p></p>
