<p><strong>The Photodynamic Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Therapy Devices</li><li>Photosensitizer Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photodynamic therapy (PDT) market consists of two primary segments: laser therapy devices and photosensitizer drugs. Laser therapy devices are specialized tools that emit specific wavelengths of light to activate photosensitizers, facilitating targeted destruction of abnormal cells. Photosensitizer drugs are chemical compounds that accumulate in diseased tissues and, upon light activation, generate reactive oxygen species, leading to cell death. Together, these components enhance the efficacy of PDT in treating various conditions, including cancer and dermatological disorders.</p></p>
