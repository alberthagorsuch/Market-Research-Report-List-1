<p><p>The thermal bonding film market comprises various types, including thermoplastic TBF, TPU film, PI thermal mucosa, and thermoset TBF. Thermoplastic TBF is versatile, used for easy processing and strong adhesion. TPU film offers flexibility and durability, ideal for applications requiring resilience. PI thermal mucosa provides high-temperature resistance, suitable for electronics and aerospace industries. Thermoset TBF is known for its strong bonding and chemical resistance, making it suitable for specialized industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs across multiple sectors.</p></p>
