<p><p>The bioplastic market includes various types such as Bio-PE, Bio-PET, regenerated cellulose, and polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA). Regenerated cellulose, sourced from wood pulp, is used in films and textiles for its biodegradability. PHA, produced by microbial fermentation, is ideal for medical and food packaging due to its biocompatibility and compostability. These bioplastics offer sustainable alternatives to conventional plastics, reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
