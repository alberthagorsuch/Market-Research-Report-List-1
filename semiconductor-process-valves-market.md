<p><p>The semiconductor process valves market includes various types critical for controlling fluid dynamics in manufacturing. Diaphragm valves provide leak-tight service, making them ideal for corrosive fluids. Bellows valves offer excellent sealing and are suitable for vacuum applications. Vacuum valves are essential for maintaining purity in semiconductor processes. Ball valves allow for quick shut-off, while butterfly valves are efficient for flow regulation. Gate valves enable on/off control in larger pipelines, angle valves redirect flow, and Teflon valves offer chemical resistance. Others include specialized designs for specific applications.</p></p>
