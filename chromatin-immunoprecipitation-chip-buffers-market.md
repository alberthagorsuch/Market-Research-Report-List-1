<p><p>The Chromatin Immunoprecipitation (ChIP) buffers market comprises several types, each serving a specific function in the ChIP process. Lysis Buffer is critical for breaking down cell membranes and releasing chromatin. Elution Buffer facilitates the release of target proteins from chromatin for analysis. High Salt Lysis Buffer is used to temporarily stabilize DNA-protein interactions while ensuring thorough lysis. Wash Buffers help remove excess, non-specifically bound materials, enhancing the purity of the final immunoprecipitate for accurate results.</p></p>
