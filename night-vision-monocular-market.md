<p><strong>The Night Vision Monocular Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The night vision monocular market is primarily divided into two types: image intensifier and thermal imaging. Image intensifier monoculars use electronic components to amplify low-light images, allowing users to see in near-dark conditions by enhancing available light. In contrast, thermal imaging monoculars detect heat signatures, enabling visibility in total darkness by converting infrared radiation into an image. Each technology serves distinct applications, catering to consumers ranging from outdoor enthusiasts to military personnel, enhancing visibility and situational awareness in low-light environments.</p></p>
