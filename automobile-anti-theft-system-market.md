<p><p>The **Automobile Anti-Theft System Market** includes various technologies designed to prevent vehicle theft. **Steering locks** physically secure the steering wheel, rendering it immobile. **Alarms** emit loud sounds and alerts when unauthorized access is detected. **Immobilizers** disable the engine unless the correct key or fob is used. **Remote Keyless Entry** allows secure, wireless locking/unlocking. **Central Locking** automates simultaneous locking of all doors. **Biometric Capture Devices** use fingerprints or other biometric data for access, offering advanced security. These systems collectively enhance vehicle protection against theft.</p></p>
