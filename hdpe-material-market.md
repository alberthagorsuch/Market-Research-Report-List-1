<p><ul><li>Blow Molding</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE material market encompasses three primary molding techniques: blow molding, injection molding, and extrusion molding. </p><p>Blow molding is used for creating hollow objects like bottles and tanks by inflating heated HDPE into molds. </p><p>Injection molding involves forcing molten HDPE into molds to produce complex shapes and sturdy items, such as containers and automotive parts. </p><p>Extrusion molding involves continuously shaping HDPE into products like pipes and sheets through a die, enabling efficient mass production of long, uniform items.</p></p>
