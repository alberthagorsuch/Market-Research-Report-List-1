<p><p>The LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) sensor market consists of various types tailored for different applications. AC LVDTs utilize alternating current for precise measurement, while DC LVDTs operate on direct current, offering enhanced stability in specific environments. Spring-loaded LVDTs or gageheads provide versatile sensing capabilities, especially in dynamic applications, due to their built-in spring mechanism. Each type caters to distinct needs in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, ensuring accurate linear position sensing across diverse conditions.</p></p>
