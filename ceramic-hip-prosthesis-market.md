<p><ul><li>Ceramics Femoral Head</li><li>Ceramics Lining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic hip prosthesis market comprises two primary types: ceramic femoral heads and ceramic linings. Ceramic femoral heads are designed to replace the ball portion of the hip joint, offering durability and low wear rates, which reduce the risk of osteolysis. Ceramic linings are utilized within the acetabular cup, providing a smooth interface for the femoral head, enhancing stability and longevity of the implant. Both types contribute to improved patient outcomes, reducing pain and enhancing mobility post-surgery.</p></p>
