<p><p>The semiconductor process market is segmented into UV-type and non-UV type tapes. UV-type tapes are designed for processes requiring ultraviolet light exposure, providing excellent adhesion and easy removal without leaving residue, ideal for photolithography applications. Non-UV type tapes, on the other hand, are used in various semiconductor manufacturing steps where UV exposure is not involved, offering diverse bonding strength and temperature resistance. Both types are critical for ensuring precision and efficiency in semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
