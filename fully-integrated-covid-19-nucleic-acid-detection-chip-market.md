<p><p>The Fully Integrated COVID-19 Nucleic Acid Detection Chip market comprises two main types: Fully Integrated Disc Chips and Isothermal Amplification Chips. Fully Integrated Disc Chips utilize a microfluidic platform that combines sample preparation, amplification, and detection into a single disc, enhancing efficiency and reducing contamination risk. In contrast, Isothermal Amplification Chips employ a constant temperature process for nucleic acid amplification, allowing for rapid and sensitive detection without the need for complex thermal cycling equipment, making them ideal for point-of-care testing.</p></p>
