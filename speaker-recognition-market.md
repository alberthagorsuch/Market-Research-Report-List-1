<p><p>Speaker recognition is a biometric technology that identifies or verifies a person's identity based on their voice characteristics. This technology is increasingly employed in various applications, including security systems, personal assistants, and customer service automation.</p><p>The Speaker Recognition Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
