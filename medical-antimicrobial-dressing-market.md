Silver alginate wound dressings are highly absorbent and ideal for managing exudate while delivering silver ions for infection control. Silver barrier dressings offer a protective layer against contaminants and microbes, enhancing wound healing by allowing gas exchange while preventing fluid loss. Each type addresses specific wound care needs effectively.</p></p>
