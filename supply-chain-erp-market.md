<p><p>The Supply Chain ERP market is primarily categorized into on-premise and cloud-based ERP systems. On-premise ERP involves software installed and maintained on local servers, offering high customization and control but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing management. In contrast, cloud-based ERP provides remote access via the internet, requiring lower initial costs and offering scalability, regular updates, and easier collaboration. Businesses choose the type based on their needs for flexibility, resources, and infrastructure preferences within supply chain management.</p></p>
