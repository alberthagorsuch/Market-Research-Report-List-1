<p><p>The Wafer Metrology and Inspection System market comprises two main types: optical-based systems and infrared type systems. Optical-based systems utilize light to measure and inspect wafer features with high precision, allowing for detailed analysis of surface topography and defects. In contrast, infrared type systems rely on thermal imaging and infrared sensors to detect variations in material properties and defects that may not be visible with traditional methods. Both technologies are essential for ensuring wafer quality in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
