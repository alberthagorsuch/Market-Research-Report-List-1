<p><strong>The Mini DNA Sequencer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nanopore sequencing</li><li>Single-Molecule Real-Time Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mini DNA Sequencer market encompasses various advanced sequencing technologies, primarily including Nanopore Sequencing and Single-Molecule Real-Time (SMRT) Sequencing. Nanopore sequencing utilizes protein nanopores to analyze DNA strands passing through them, enabling real-time data capture and the analysis of long reads. In contrast, SMRT sequencing employs real-time observation of DNA polymerase activity, allowing for the sequencing of single molecules with high accuracy. Both technologies enhance accessibility and efficiency in genomic research and diagnostics.</p></p>
