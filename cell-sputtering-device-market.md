<p><ul><li>Vertically Transporting Substrates Sputtering Device</li><li>Horizontally Transporting Substrates Sputtering Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell sputtering device market is categorized into two main types based on substrate transport orientation: vertically transporting substrates and horizontally transporting substrates. Vertically transporting substrates sputtering devices utilize a vertical configuration, which allows for efficient material deposition and improved uniformity on the substrate surface. In contrast, horizontally transporting substrates sputtering devices operate in a horizontal setup, facilitating a different approach to material coating and often accommodating larger substrates. Both types cater to varying production requirements in semiconductor and materials engineering sectors.</p></p>
