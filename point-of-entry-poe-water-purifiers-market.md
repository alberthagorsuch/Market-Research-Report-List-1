<p><p>The Point-of-Entry (POE) water purifier market encompasses various technologies for water treatment at the source. Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems utilize a semipermeable membrane to remove contaminants by applying pressure, producing purified water. Ultrafiltration (UF) membrane systems use a finer membrane to separate particles and bacteria from water without requiring high pressure. Other technologies may include carbon filtration, distillation, and UV purification. Each type targets specific water quality needs, catering to diverse consumer preferences and regional water conditions.</p></p>
