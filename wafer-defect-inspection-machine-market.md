<p><ul><li>Optical Inspection</li><li>Laser Inspection</li><li>Electron Beam Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer defect inspection machine market comprises three primary types: optical inspection, laser inspection, and electron beam inspection. Optical inspection uses light to detect surface defects by analyzing reflections, offering fast and non-destructive evaluation. Laser inspection employs focused laser beams to highlight minute irregularities, providing high-resolution data. Electron beam inspection utilizes focused electron beams to achieve exceptional accuracy, capable of identifying nanoscale defects, making it ideal for advanced semiconductor applications. Each method serves crucial roles in ensuring wafer quality and yield improvement.</p></p>
