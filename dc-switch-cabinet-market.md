<p><p>The DC switch cabinet market is divided into low voltage and high voltage switch cabinets. Low voltage switch cabinets are designed for applications operating at voltages up to 1,000 volts, providing circuit protection, distribution, and control for various electrical systems. Both types are essential for effective power management and infrastructure stability.</p></p>
