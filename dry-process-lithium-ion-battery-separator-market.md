<p><p>The Dry Process Lithium-ion Battery Separator Market consists of two primary types: Dry Process Single Pull and Dry Process Double Pull separators. The Single Pull method involves creating a thin, continuous separator film in a single operation, offering simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, the Double Pull method entails a two-step process, allowing for enhanced control over film thickness and properties, which can improve battery performance. Both types cater to the growing demand for lightweight, high-performance battery solutions in various applications.</p></p>
