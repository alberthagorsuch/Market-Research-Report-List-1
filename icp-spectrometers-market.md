<p><p>ICP spectrometers are classified into two primary market types: sequential and simultaneous. Sequential ICP spectrometers analyze one element at a time, offering high sensitivity and resolution, making them suitable for precise applications. In contrast, simultaneous ICP spectrometers measure multiple elements concurrently, providing faster analysis and higher throughput. This dual approach enables users to select instruments based on their specific analytical needs, balancing between precision and efficiency while addressing various industries, including environmental monitoring, metallurgy, and materials science.</p></p>
