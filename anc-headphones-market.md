<p><p>The ANC (Active Noise Cancelling) headphones market can be categorized into three main types: In-Ear, On-Ear, and Over-Ear. In-Ear headphones are compact and fit snugly within the ear canal, providing excellent portability and isolation. On-Ear headphones rest on the ears, offering a balance between comfort and sound quality while remaining relatively portable. Over-Ear headphones completely envelop the ears, delivering superior sound quality and enhanced noise cancellation, making them ideal for immersive listening experiences in various environments.</p></p>
