<p><p>The Mastic Waterproof Coating Market is primarily divided into two types: One Component and Two Component. One Component mastic coatings are ready-to-use products that require no mixing, offering ease of application and decreased labor time. In contrast, Two Component mastic coatings consist of a base and a hardener that must be mixed prior to application. This type generally provides superior adhesion and durability, making it suitable for demanding environments. Both types cater to different requirements based on performance and application ease.</p></p>
