<p><strong>The Radioactive Waste Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Level Waste</li><li>Intermediate Level Waste</li><li>High Level Waste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radioactive Waste Management System market is categorized into three main types: Low Level Waste (LLW), Intermediate Level Waste (ILW), and High Level Waste (HLW). LLW comprises materials with low radioactivity, typically disposed of in near-surface facilities. ILW contains higher radioactivity and requires more complex handling and disposal, often stored in engineered facilities. HLW, produced from nuclear reactors, is highly radioactive and generates significant heat, necessitating deep geological repositories for safe long-term storage and management.</p></p>
