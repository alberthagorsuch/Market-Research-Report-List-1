<p><ul><li>Gas Ionization Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor Detectors</li><li>Scintillation Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable radiation detectors market comprises three main types: gas ionization detectors, semiconductor detectors, and scintillation detectors. Gas ionization detectors use gases to measure radiation levels by ionizing gas molecules. Semiconductor detectors leverage semiconductor materials to detect radiation, offering high resolution and energy discrimination. Scintillation detectors employ materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, allowing for sensitive detection. Each type serves distinct applications in fields such as safety, security, and scientific research, catering to varied detection needs and environments.</p></p>
