<p><p>Single Selective Pallet Racking comprises two primary market types: Roll Form Type and Structural Steel Type. Roll Form Type features lightweight, bolted components made from cold-rolled metal, allowing for flexibility and ease of installation. It's ideal for warehouses with varying load requirements. In contrast, Structural Steel Type utilizes heavier, welded steel beams, offering superior durability and load capacity, making it suitable for high-demand environments. Both types enhance storage efficiency, accessibility, and organization in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
