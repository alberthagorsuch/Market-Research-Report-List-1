<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Ion Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Ion Pair Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra High Performance Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) market includes several specific types of chromatography. Ion Exchange Chromatography focuses on separating ions and polar molecules based on their charge. Ion Exclusion Chromatography is used to separate ions from neutral molecules, primarily targeting low molecular weight compounds. Ion Pair Chromatography combines ionic interactions with traditional techniques, enhancing the separation of complex mixtures. Each type plays a critical role in analyzing various biological, pharmaceutical, and environmental samples efficiently and accurately.</p></p>
