<p><p>The cables for endoscopes market includes three primary types: lighting cables, data cables, and hybrid cables. </p><p>Lighting cables transmit illumination from an external light source to the endoscope, enhancing visibility during procedures. Data cables facilitate the transfer of video and imaging data from the endoscope to imaging systems, ensuring high-quality visuals for diagnosis. Hybrid cables combine both lighting and data transmission capabilities in a single cable, offering convenience and reducing clutter in medical setups while maintaining performance efficiency.</p></p>
