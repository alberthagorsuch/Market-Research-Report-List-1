<p><p>The HVAC market encompasses various system types, including Direct Expansion (DX) Systems and Central Air Conditioning Systems. DX systems operate by directly cooling air through refrigerant evaporation, making them ideal for smaller spaces and applications that require localized temperature control. In contrast, Central Air Conditioning Systems provide cooling and heating to larger areas or entire buildings by distributing conditioned air through ducts, offering more comprehensive climate control solutions suitable for homes, offices, and commercial spaces.</p></p>
