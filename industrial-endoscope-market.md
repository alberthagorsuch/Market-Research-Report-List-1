<p><p>The industrial endoscope market includes various types, notably fiberscopes and rigid borescopes. Fiberscopes utilize flexible optical fibers, allowing inspection in confined and complex spaces, making them ideal for troubleshooting and maintenance in diverse industries. Rigid borescopes, on the other hand, feature a fixed design and are used for direct visual inspection in areas that require precise alignment and stability. Both types enhance equipment safety, reduce downtime, and improve maintenance efficiency in sectors such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
