<p><ul><li>Through-hull</li><li>Surface-mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater yacht light market primarily features two types: through-hull and surface-mount lights. Through-hull lights are installed directly into the yacht's hull, providing a sleek, integrated look and a wide beam angle for optimal underwater illumination. In contrast, surface-mount lights are attached to the outer hull, making installation simpler and often allowing for flexible positioning. Both types enhance nighttime visibility and aesthetics, attracting marine life and improving safety during nighttime activities.</p></p>
