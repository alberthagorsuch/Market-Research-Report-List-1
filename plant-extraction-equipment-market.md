<p><p>The plant extraction equipment market comprises three primary types: extraction equipment, concentration equipment, and separation and purification equipment. Extraction equipment facilitates the initial process of obtaining bioactive compounds from plant materials using solvents or pressurization techniques. Concentration equipment is used to concentrate these extracts by removing excess solvents or water, enhancing potency. Separation and purification equipment further refines the extracts, isolating specific compounds to achieve desired purity levels for use in pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
